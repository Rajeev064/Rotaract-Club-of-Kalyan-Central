ABOUT US

Welcome to the club! We are Rotaract Club of Kalyan Central, a youth organization, working towards the betterment of our world, effort by effort, shoulder to shoulder. Rotaract, the youth wing of Rotary International lives up to its motto of “fellowship through service” which has been our very aim from the very beginning.
